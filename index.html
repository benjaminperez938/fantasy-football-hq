OFFICIAL RULES & REGULATIONS · 25-26
Welcome to Fantasy Football! Our mission here is to have fun and compete for cash prizes. Enjoy longtime rivalries and root for your favorite teams as we enter the gridiron together!
I. LEAGUE OVERVIEW
1. Commissioner: Benjamin P.
2. Co-Commissioner: TBA
3. Treasurer: TBA
4. Platform: ESPN Fantasy
5. Format: 10 teams, expanding and capped at 14 next year
6. Head-to-head matchups · PPR or Standard scoring
7. Weekly matchups: Thursday – Monday
II. ROSTER SETTINGS
1. Roster Size: 10–14
2. Waiver System: Rolling — priority given in reverse rank order
3. Trade Deadline: Beginning of Thursday Night Football each week
4. Playoff Format: TBA
III. ENTRY FEES & PRIZES
1. Entry Fee: $30 — due by draft day
2. 1st Place: $150 + Trophy (to be returned)
3. 2nd Place: $75
4. 3rd Place: $50
5. All others receive 20% buy-in back (~$5)
6. Weekly Roulette winner earns a $5 prize payout
7. Last place receives a Loser Challenge
8. Payment via Venmo or Zelle
IV. DRAFT RULES
1. Draft Type: Snake (Auction format planned for future)
2. Draft Date & Time: TBA
3. Time Per Pick: 1.5 minutes
4. Draft Order: Decided by Order Challenge — see Important Dates
V. SEASON RULES
1. No trading past the trade deadline — trades close at the start of Thursday Night Football each week
2. Waiver order decided by manager rank in reverse order (lowest to highest)
3. Lineup window closes at the start of every Thursday Night Football game
4. Tanking, collusion, or inactivity may result in strikes, suspensions, or team restructuring/keeper privileges revoked
VI. WEEKLY ROULETTE
1. Every Wednesday at 12:00 PM PST a category is spun — highest scorer in that category wins the $5 weekly payout
2. Categories include: QB, RB1, RB2, WR1, WR2, TE, Kicker, D/ST, Bench Bum, Overall Bench, Overall Non-Bench, and combo categories
3. First spin of the week locks the result — no re-spins without a majority league vote (4 of 6 members)
4. Commissioner override PIN required to unlock a re-spin after a passing vote
5. Once locked, no authorized user may spin again that week
VII. KEEPERS & CONTRACTS
1. Contracts for extended participation begin in Year 5
2. Planned expansion to 12–14 teams
3. Keeper system starting Year 5: keep 1 player, costs a 1st round draft pick
VIII. CODE OF CONDUCT
1. Respectful communication is expected from all members at all times
2. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks — in the app or any league communication
3. Keep the group chat fun and friendly — trash talk is welcome, but know the line
4. 3-strike policy enforced by the commissioner — violations may result in strikes, suspension, or removal
5. Commissioner rulings are final unless overturned by a majority league vote
6. If the commissioner cannot be reached, contact the Co-Commissioner or Treasurer
COMMISSIONER: BENJAMIN P. · FANTASY LEAGUE HQ · 25-26
